About
ME

I was raised around tech so picking up software skills was part of babies first steps, you could say. I'm began as a self taught coder; I had my first blog in my teens. I have multiple years of data analytics experience -- using SQL and other database tools, and, plenty of experience with web design, UX design, and, XR development. I love each subset of computer science, and, find that my varied and overarching tech background has allowed me to have a ‘big picture’ philosophy when working on any project. It's my belief that being the proxy for technical diversification has served as a great aid in collaboration between teams as I am able to properly translate ideas and interests.
